Termite Treatment

Generally speaking, termites eat away at wooden items like doors, windows, beds, wardrobes, etc. Termites consume wood as well as other cellulose-based materials such as paper. Like a result, they consume wood as food and eat away at wooden objects. Numerous studies indicate that termites cause billions of rupees worth of damage to wooden and other items. As a result, you can appreciate how deadly termites may be if they exist in your property.

Fumigation

Fumigation is the use of a lethal gas to exterminate pests within an enclosed space. There are two types of fumigation. One is to seal the structure with plastics, tape, or others, and the other is to enclose the structure with vinyl-coated nylon tents.
